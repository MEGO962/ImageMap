Zarqa: The Industrial Pulse of Jordan

Zarqa, the bustling industrial heart of Jordan, is a city where tradition meets progress. Known for its factories, workshops, and vibrant markets, Zarqa plays a crucial role in the country's economic growth. The city’s name, derived from the Arabic word for "blue," hints at its historic connection to the Zarqa River. Although urban and industrial in nature, Zarqa is also a cultural hub where visitors can explore its rich heritage, traditional cuisine, and friendly neighborhoods.

Stroll through its dynamic streets, visit the historic railway station, and experience the authentic spirit of a city that embodies resilience and growth. Zarqa is not just a place—it's an experience.
