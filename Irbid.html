Irbid: The Heart of the North

Irbid, located in the northern part of Jordan, is a vibrant city rich in history and culture. Known as the "City of Knowledge," Irbid is home to several educational institutions, including the prestigious Yarmouk University. The city offers a blend of traditional and modern influences, making it an exciting destination for visitors. Explore the bustling streets, historic sites, and beautiful parks that define this northern gem. Irbid is also known for its delicious food, warm hospitality, and as a hub for regional commerce and trade.
