Al-Balqa: The Historic Hills of Jordan

Al-Balqa, with its historical capital Salt, is a region filled with scenic beauty and rich history. Known for its yellow sandstone architecture, charming old streets, and welcoming atmosphere, Salt reflects the timeless spirit of Al-Balqa.

The region is surrounded by lush green hills and offers stunning views of the valleys below. A visit to Al-Balqa is a journey through Jordan's rich cultural heritage, where tradition meets natural beauty, and history comes alive in every corner.
