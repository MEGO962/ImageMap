Amman: The Heart of Jordan

Amman, the bustling capital of Jordan, is a city where history meets modernity. Known for its iconic white stone buildings, Amman is a city of contrasts, blending ancient ruins like the Amman Citadel and the Roman Theater with modern skyscrapers and lively markets.

From the vibrant souks of Downtown to the upscale neighborhoods of Abdoun, Amman offers a rich cultural experience. Its diverse food scene, warm hospitality, and stunning views from its seven hills make it a must-visit destination in the Middle East.
